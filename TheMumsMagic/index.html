About Us

When it comes to food, love and care , no one can beat our darling mothers. One of the best ways in which they express their love for us is through food. The Mum's Magic is a food blog where we share flavorful and healthy(mostly:|) recipes that we love to make and eat in actual every day life. All recipes here are home cooked and tested by an indian mother having almost 20-25 years of experience in cooking which means you can totally rely on us. This blog is a humble attempt at sharing our passion for cooking and you will find a lot of tips and tricks that can save your day. So be with us :)
